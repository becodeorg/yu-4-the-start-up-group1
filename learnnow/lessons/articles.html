Articles
If you're trying to learn articles you will find some useful resources including a course about definite and indefinite articles
Try to concentrate on the lesson and notice the pattern that occurs each time the word changes its place.

Learning the articles is very important because its structure is used in every day conversations.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the language.
But first we need to know what the role of articles is in the structure of the grammar.
Articles are words that combine with a noun to indicate the type of reference being made by the noun.
Generally articles specify the grammatical definiteness of the noun.

Enjoy the lesson!

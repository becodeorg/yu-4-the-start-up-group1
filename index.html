OUR GOAL.

Our goal at dutch4free is to help everyone learn Dutch on an easy way! After registering to our website you get access to our learning enviroment. In this enviroment you can follow several lessons to learn Dutch.

You start with easier lessons and while making progress the difficulty will rise. The tempo is yours if you want to get deeper or learn something again you can always decide.

In the end of the 4 month journey you will have learned a lot more then you could ever imagine. When you are learning the lessons and you feel lost there are always others to help you, we will be there for you. The only thing that we ask from you is to never lose hope!

Prepositions
Learning the Prepositions is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the language.
But first we need to know what the role of Prepositions is in the structure of the grammar.

Flemish prepositions link nouns, pronouns and phrases to other words in a sentence.
The word or phrase that the preposition introduces is called the object of the preposition.

Here are some examples:
Prepositionsvoorzetsels
inside the housein het huis
outside the carbuiten de auto
with memet mij/me
without himzonder hem
under the tableonder de tafel
after tomorrowna morgen
before sunsetvoor zonsondergang
but I'm busymaar ik ben bezig


Notice the structure of the Prepositions.

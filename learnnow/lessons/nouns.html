Nouns
If you're trying to learn Nouns you will find some useful resources including a course about Nouns and Words.
Try to concentrate on the lesson and notice the pattern that occurs each time the word changes its place.

Learning the Nouns is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the language.
But first we need to know what the role of Nouns is in the structure of the grammar.

Nouns are words used to name a person, animal, place, thing, or abstract ideas.
Nouns are usually the most important part of vocabulary.

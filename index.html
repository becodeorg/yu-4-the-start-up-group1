OUR GOAL.

Our goal at dutch4free is to help you learn Dutch on an easy way! After registering to our website you get access to our unique learning enviroment. In this enviroment you can follow several excercises to learn Dutch.

You start with easy excercises and while you make progress the difficulty will rise. The tempo is yours if you want to get deeper or learn something again you can always decide.

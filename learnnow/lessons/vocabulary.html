Flemish Vocabulary
If you're trying to learn vocabulary you will find some useful resources including a course about vocabulary and expressions.
Try to concentrate on the lesson and notice the pattern that occurs each time the word changes its place.

Learning the Flemish Vocabulary is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the Flemish language.
But first we need to know what the role of Vocabulary is in the structure of the grammar in Flemish.

Vocabulary is the set of words you should be familiar with.
A vocabulary usually grows and evolves with age, and serves as a useful and fundamental tool for communication and acquiring knowledge.

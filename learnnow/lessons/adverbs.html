Adverbs
Learning the Adverbs is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the Flemish language.
But first we need to know what the role of Adverbs is in the structure of the grammar.

Adverbs are part of speech.
Generally they're words that modify any part of language other than a noun. Adverbs can modify verbs, adjectives (including numbers), clauses, sentences and other adverbs.

Negation
If you're trying to learn negation you will find some useful resources including a course about Negation and negative expressions.
Try to concentrate on the lesson and notice the pattern that occurs each time the word changes its place.

Learning the negation is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the language.
But first we need to know what the role of Negation is in the structure of the grammar.

Negation is the process that turns an affirmative statement (I am happy) into its opposite denial (I am not happy).

Here are some examples:

Negationontkenning
he is not herehij is hier niet
that is not my bookdat is mijn boek niet
do not enterga niet binnen


Notice the structure of the Negation.

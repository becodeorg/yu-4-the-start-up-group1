Adjectives
Learning the Adjectives is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation.
The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the language.
But first we need to know what the role of Adjectives is in the structure of the grammar.

Adjectives are words that describe or modify another person or thing in the sentence.

Here are some examples:

adjectivesbijvoeglijke naamwoorden
a green treeeen groene boom
a tall buildingeen hoog gebouw
a very old maneen heel oude man
the old red househet oud rood huis/ het oude rode huis [last only in written language]
a very nice friendeen heel lieve vriend/vriendin


Notice the structure of the Adjectives.
